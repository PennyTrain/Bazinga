How to Play BAZINGA!

BAZINGA is a twist on your average Rock Paper Scissor game and adds 2 extra elements, lizard and spock.

  • Scissors cuts paper
  • Paper covers rock
  • Rock crushes lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes scissors
  • Scissors decapitates lizard
  • Lizard eats paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes rock
  • Rock crushes scissors
